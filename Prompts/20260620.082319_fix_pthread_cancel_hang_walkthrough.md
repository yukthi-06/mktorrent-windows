## Decisions made during implementation
- Replaced the use of `pthread_cancel` on the progress thread with a thread-safe `stop_progress` flag polled by the progress thread.
- This avoids deadlocks/hangs on `pthread_join(print_progress_thread)` which happen on Windows due to buggy asynchronous thread cancellation support under `winpthreads`.
